What Is TRE®?

Your Nervous System's
Natural Reset Button

TRE® — Tension and Trauma Release Exercises — is a set of simple exercises designed to help the body release deep muscular patterns of stress, tension and trauma stored in the nervous system.

Developed by Dr. David Berceli, a trauma intervention specialist, TRE® works by activating the psoas muscle — the deep hip flexor that is at the centre of the body's stress response. When activated in a safe, controlled way, this muscle triggers neurogenic tremors: gentle, involuntary shaking that the nervous system uses to discharge stored tension.

Animals in the wild do this naturally after threatening situations. Humans have largely lost this capacity — but it can be safely reactivated. TRE® is the bridge back to that natural self-regulation.

The Process

How TRE® Works

Simple exercises, profound results. No special equipment needed.

1

Prepare the Body

Lie on a yoga mat in comfortable clothing. A short series of physical exercises gently fatigue the leg muscles and wake up the nervous system.

2

Activate the Psoas

Specific movements activate the psoas — the deepest core muscle and the body's primary stress-response muscle — creating the conditions for release.

3

Allow the Tremors

Natural neurogenic tremors — gentle shaking — begin. The body's own intelligence guides the release. You remain fully conscious and in control throughout.

4

Rest & Integrate

After the session, rest and notice the shift: a calmer nervous system, lighter body and greater sense of ease. Integration continues in the days that follow.

The Science

Psychology. Neurology. Physiology.

TRE® draws on decades of research across multiple disciplines.

Polyvagal Theory

TRE® works in harmony with Dr. Stephen Porges' Polyvagal Theory — moving the nervous system out of the fight-or-flight (sympathetic) response and into the calm, connected state governed by the ventral vagus nerve.

The Psoas Muscle

The psoas is directly connected to the body's survival response. When activated through TRE®, it can release tension that has been held — sometimes for decades — at the deepest muscular level.

Neurogenic Tremors

All mammals use neurogenic tremors to discharge stress after threatening situations. TRE® simply reactivates this capacity in humans — allowing the body to complete stress cycles that were interrupted.

Safety & Suitability

Is TRE® Right for Me?

TRE® is generally safe and suitable for the vast majority of people. It is used by people of all ages and fitness levels — from corporate executives to retirees.

We recommend consulting a healthcare professional first if you have:

  • Current serious psychological conditions
  • Recent surgery or significant physical injury
  • Pregnancy
  • Active epilepsy

TRE® never requires you to discuss or relive traumatic experiences. It works entirely through the body's own intelligence — making it accessible for people who find talk-based approaches difficult.

After 3–4 supervised sessions with Isabelle, most clients are able to practice independently at home — gaining a lifelong self-regulation tool they can use whenever needed.
