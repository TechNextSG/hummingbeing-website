What Is Somatic Coaching?

The Intelligence
Your Body Has Always Had

Most people have spent 12–20 years training their intellect. They know how to think, analyse, strategise and plan. But the body — the source of intuition, emotional regulation, relational attunement and presence — receives almost no training at all.

Somatic Coaching changes that. Using guided questions and structured practices, Isabelle directs your attention inward — helping you develop sensory awareness, discernment and the capacity to respond (rather than react) from a place of genuine choice.

This is not about understanding yourself better intellectually. As the practice puts it: "Understanding is insufficient. It really is learned through experience and practice." Somatic Coaching is the gym for your inner life.

A Different Kind of Coaching

Beyond the Talking Cure

Traditional coaching works from the neck up. Somatic coaching works with the whole person.

Embodied Awareness

You learn to read your body's signals — the tension in your shoulders, the tightening in your chest, the ease in your breath — as a rich source of information about your inner state.

In-the-Moment Access

Unlike purely cognitive coaching, somatic work gives you tools accessible in real time — in a tense meeting, a difficult conversation, a moment of overwhelm — not just in hindsight.

Breaks Old Patterns

Many of our reactive patterns were wired in long before we could choose them. Somatic coaching addresses these patterns at the level where they actually live — in the body.

Regulates the Nervous System

By developing body awareness, you naturally become better at regulating your nervous system — moving fluidly between activation and calm rather than getting stuck in chronic stress states.

Like Learning an Instrument

The somatic learning model mirrors music training — foundational practices build skill and capacity over time. Progress is tangible, measurable and deeply personal.

Sustainable Self-Awareness

The skills you develop are yours permanently. Unlike a course that ends, somatic awareness becomes woven into how you navigate every area of life.

Honest About Commitment

Real Transformation Takes Time

Somatic Coaching is not a quick fix and Isabelle will always be honest about that. Genuine transformation of embodied patterns — the ones wired in over years and decades — requires patience, practice and commitment.

Clients typically commit to 6–12 sessions or longer depending on their goals and depth of work. Many find the journey so valuable they continue well beyond that.

If you are looking for a profound, lasting shift — not just temporary relief — Somatic Coaching may be exactly what you need.
