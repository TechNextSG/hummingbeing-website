Her Story

From Fortune 100 HR Leader
to Somatic Practitioner

Isabelle brings over 25 years of experience as a Senior HR Business Partner and regional Talent Management leader in Fortune 100 companies — followed by 5 years as an entrepreneur across Japan and Singapore.

Her decades in high-performance corporate environments gave her a first-hand understanding of the personal and health costs that ambition can carry. She experienced the stress, the burnout and the disconnection that so many high achievers know quietly but rarely discuss.

That journey — and a deep curiosity about the body's role in human transformation — led her to somatic practices. Today, she brings the credibility of a seasoned business leader and the expertise of a trained somatic practitioner together in a unique and powerful offering.

A Truly Global Journey

9 Countries. 40+ Nationalities. One Mission.

Isabelle has lived and worked across an extraordinary range of cultures — including the United States, France, Russia, Japan, Singapore and multiple other countries in Asia and Europe.

This depth of cross-cultural exposure has shaped her ability to connect with clients from all walks of life — adapting her language, approach and style to whatever serves the individual in front of her.

She has collaborated with colleagues and clients representing 40+ nationalities across diverse religious, gender and age backgrounds. Her somatic practice is built on this foundation of genuine cultural respect and curiosity.
