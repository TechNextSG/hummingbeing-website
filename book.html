Common Questions

Before You Book

Answers to the questions Isabelle hears most often.

Do I need any previous experience with somatic practices?

No experience is necessary at all. The practices are designed to meet you exactly where you are — whether you are completely new to bodywork or have years of experience with related disciplines.

Do I need to talk about my trauma or past experiences?

Absolutely not. One of the most significant advantages of somatic work — particularly TRE® — is that it does not require you to verbally process or relive past events. The body releases what it needs to release without narrative.

How many sessions will I need?

This varies by individual and goal. For TRE®, most clients are ready to practice independently after 3–4 supervised sessions. For Somatic Coaching, clients typically commit to 6–12 sessions. Isabelle will give you an honest assessment during your discovery call.

Can sessions be done online?

Yes — all three modalities can be delivered effectively online via video call. Many of Isabelle's clients across Asia Pacific and internationally access sessions this way. In-person is available in Singapore and Japan.

Is this available for corporate teams?

Yes. Isabelle offers team resilience programmes and group TRE® sessions for organisations. These are particularly valuable for leadership teams navigating change, stress or high-pressure performance environments. Contact us to discuss options.
